# Rules

### Controls

## Movement
WASD - Move player  
SHIFT (hold) - Run (faster, but noisy)  
C (hold) - Sneak (slower, but silent)  

## Combat & Interaction
LMB - Attack / Shoot  
E - Interact (Open/Close doors, Pick up weapon, Use elevator, Silent kill)  
G - Drop current weapon  
ESC - Pause game  

## Debug Mode
F1 - Toggle debug overlay  

### Gameplay Guide

## Stealth
Enemies have a field of view and can hear sounds.  
Running and shooting creates noise that alerts nearby enemies.  
Sneaking (holding C) makes you completely silent, allowing you to sneak past enemies.  

## Doors
Press E to open or close doors.  
Closed doors block enemy vision and muffle sounds, giving you a chance to hide.  

## Silent Kills
While in Sneak mode, approach an enemy from behind undetected.  
When close enough, press E to perform a silent kill.  

## Weapons
Different weapons have different fire modes, damage, and noise.  
Drop your weapon with G to pick up a new one from the ground with E.  

### Victory Conditions

## Level 1: Entrance
Find a way to reach one of the elevators in the lower area to escape.  

## Level 2: Halls
Defeat all enemies blocking your way through the corridors.  

## Level 3: Party
Locate the VIP rooms and defeat the party champion.
